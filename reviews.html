Movie: Inception

Rating: 5/5

A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. Inception immerses viewers into a world of dreams within dreams, led by a stellar cast including Leonardo DiCaprio and Joseph Gordon-Levitt. The complex narrative and breathtaking visuals make it a masterpiece of modern cinema.

Movie: Titanic

Rating: 4.8/5

A timeless romance directed by James Cameron. Titanic tells the story of Jack and Rose, whose love transcends social class and faces the ultimate test aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. With stunning visuals, a haunting score, and memorable performances by Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet, it remains an unforgettable cinematic experience.

Movie: Avengers: Endgame

Rating: 5/5

The culmination of over a decade of storytelling in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Directed by the Russo brothers, Avengers: Endgame brings together Earth's mightiest heroes for a final, epic battle against Thanos. Packed with emotional moments, breathtaking action, and satisfying resolutions, it serves as a fitting end to a remarkable saga.
